About

Chinatown Tattoo

Chinatown Tattoo first opened in New York City's Chinatown in 2008. We provide small introductory tattoos from existing designs or hand drawn and personally designed tattoos from the artist themselves.

Location

We are currently located at 5824 8th Ave 2nd Floor Suite 202, Brooklyn, NY 11220. The closest train station is the 8 Ave N train which is a 4 minute walk away. Click here to figure out how to get to us!

Appointments

Consulting Appointments: Consulting Appointments are FREE. They are for you and the artist to talk about the design and price. Consulting Appointments are 30 mintues long sessions and can be booked through our Artist Page.

Tattoo Appointment: Tattoo Appointments can only be booked during your Consulting Appointment. If you require multiple Tattoo Appointments, then the next one will be book at the end of your current Tattoo Appointment.
